Version 0.1.5 June 24, 2026

Full KJV release and early stability fixes

Theosis now includes the full King James Bible in Script, with all 66 books available for chapter and verse reading. This release also focuses on correcting early post-release issues where Scribe and Script were not fully wired in the browser sidebar.

  • Loaded the complete KJV text into the extension.
  • Expanded Script from a limited preview to all 66 books.
  • Connected the in-page Scribe panel to the live AI backend.
  • Improved the release package structure for Chrome Web Store updates.
Post-release development June 24, 2026

What we are working on next

The next phase is about making Theosis feel more reliable, useful, and spiritually meaningful in everyday browsing. The immediate priorities are improving Scribe, strengthening Exegete Me, and making Script easier to use.

  • Clearer user experience inside the sidebar.
  • Better theological response formatting.
  • More visible product status and community communication.
  • Early planning for future accounts, saved notes, and deeper study tools.
Version 0.1.0 June 23, 2026

Theosis begins public release

The first public version of Theosis launched as an AI faith companion for the browser, introducing Scribe, Exegete Me, Script, and a mission-centered new tab experience.
